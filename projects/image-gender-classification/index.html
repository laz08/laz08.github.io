About

The motivation of this project is derived from multiple factors.

To summarize, we have always worked with numerical or categorical data. But having technology nowadays such as Facial recognition, we asked ourselves: how does machine learning work applied to images? And so, this project was born.

This study was done as an assignment for the subject called Machine Learning (ML) for Facultat d’Informatica de Barcelona at Universitat Politecnica de Catalunya (FIB - UPC). This subject is from the Master in Innovation and Research in Informatics at said university.

Steps taken

Although it is advise that you just read the report (found here), we basically applied these steps:

  1. Research how facial features could be extracted from images.
  2. More specifically, research feature extraction on images of faces.
  3. Apply Local Binary Pattern (aka LBP) to our data. LBP is the method chosen to convert the features into numerical data.
  4. Split our data 80%/20% as training and test sets, respectively.
  5. Take note of the accuracy and log loss when classifying with:
    • K Nearest Neighbors
    • Support Vector Machines
    • Convolutional Neural Networks
  6. Compare the results.
